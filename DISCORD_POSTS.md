# Graffito Workshop Discord Posts

Copy the contents of each fenced block into the named Discord channel. The
channel references use Discord's native `<#channel-id>` syntax, so they become
clickable channel mentions when posted.

Recommended posting order:

1. Rules
2. Roles and notifications
3. FAQ and resources
4. Graffito-Editor news
5. Announcements
6. Welcome

After posting, pin each message in its channel.

## `#rules`

```text
# Graffito Workshop Rules

1. **Treat people well.** No harassment, hate speech, personal attacks, threats, or targeted hostility.

2. **Keep things readable and relevant.** Use the appropriate channel, avoid spam, and do not repeatedly advertise or derail conversations.

3. **Do not distribute copyrighted game data.** No disc images, ROMs, retail archives, keys, or extracted Nintendo assets. Share source code, documentation, original work, and patch files that require users to provide their own legally obtained copy.

4. **Keep the server safe for a general audience.** No explicit sexual content, graphic material, scams, malware, or deliberately unsafe downloads.

5. **Give useful technical context.** For help or bug reports, include the version, reproduction steps, relevant logs, and what you already tried. Remove personal information from logs first.

6. **Respect creators and researchers.** Credit work, do not misrepresent another person's findings, and honor project licenses.

7. **Do not impersonate staff or claim Nintendo affiliation.** This is an unofficial fan community.

8. **Follow Discord's Terms of Service and staff direction.** Moderators may act on disruptive behavior not exhaustively covered above.
```

## `#faq-and-resources`

```text
# Quick Start and Resources

## Is Graffito-Editor finished?

No. Graffito-Editor is experimental, unfinished software under active development. Builds and automated tests do not guarantee that every runtime or visual path works correctly.

## Can I upload my game files here?

No. Do not upload retail disc images, extracted archives, encryption keys, or copyrighted Nintendo assets. Use legal patch-based workflows.

## Where should I ask for help?

Use <#1528887490860290159> for general help, <#1528887499609604140> for Graffito-Editor usage and discussion, and <#1528887508895535237> for reproducible defects.

## What makes a good bug report?

Include the Graffito-Editor version or commit, operating system, exact reproduction steps, expected behavior, actual behavior, and sanitized logs or screenshots.
```
